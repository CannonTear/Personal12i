Jack Cannon is a nationally recognized designer. Information on him can be found around the site.




Jack, at the age of fifteen, has over 2,000 live performance technical experiences. In the past year, Jack worked on two national premieres and three world premiere professional theatre productions. Jack specializes in festival and theatre lighting design, programming, and operation. He also has worked as a professional theatre stage manager. Jack has professional credits in music production and mastering, live sound engineering, master carpentry, projection mapping, technical direction, production management, and equipment repair. Jack is professionally trained in all ETC control equipment as well as MA Lighting grandMA, MA2, MA3, and Dot2 consoles. He is also trained in Capture drafting and live/pre-visualization. He has professional affiliations with the International Association of Lighting Designers, Actors Equity Association, Illinois Theatre Association, and the International Thespian Society.
